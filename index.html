Tabloids

20 cards
Tabloid

Box Office Bomb Scare

Place 1 Delay token on any Movie in any opponent's Studio Lot.
Tabloid — headhunt

Rival Offer

During Sabotage Window: automatically take one Crew from rolling player's declared subset. That Crew enters your Studio Lot.
Tabloid — headhunt

Star Power Bid

Play when any opponent wins a Table Bid. Roll off (2 D6 each). Win = take one Crew from their lot. Lose = they draw 1 card.
Tabloid

Strike!

All players (including you) must discard 1 Crew card from their Studio Lot.
Tabloid

Hostile Audition

Look at the top 5 cards of the Crew Deck. Take 1 into your hand. Shuffle the rest back.
Tabloid

The Scoop

Look at any one opponent's hand. You may not reveal the contents to other players.
Tabloid

Market Crash

Force any player to reroll their income die using the Standard D3 instead of their chosen die.
Tabloid

Award Season

All Nominated films across all Studio Lots gain +1 Financial Value for end game scoring only.
Tabloid

Bidding War

Take any Movie from the Center Row and immediately open a Table Bid on it.
Tabloid

Casting Couch

Steal 1 Bid from each opponent who has at least one Bad PR card on any of their Crew.
Tabloid

Streaming Deal

Collect Bids equal to any one Movie's Financial Value without releasing it. Place 1 Delay token on that Movie.
Tabloid

Walk of Fame

Choose one Crew card in your Studio Lot. It permanently gains +1 die on all Film Rolls.
Tabloid

Director's Cut

Immediately attempt a Film Roll on any Movie in your Studio Lot for free (no AP cost).
Tabloid

Popcorn Moment

All players draw 1 card from either deck of their choice.
Tabloid

The Merger

Swap one Crew from your Studio Lot with one Crew from any opponent's Studio Lot. Neither player rolls.
Tabloid

Press Junket

Remove all Bad PR cards from all Crew in your Studio Lot. Discard the removed Bad PR cards.
Tabloid

Box Office Records

Your next successful Film Roll earns double its Financial Value in Bids. Does not affect end game scoring.
Tabloid

The Boycott

Declare one genre. No Movie of that genre may enter a Table Bid this round. Drawn Movies of that genre go to the Center Row.
Tabloid

Greenlight

Take any Movie from the Center Row into your Studio Lot for free. Attempt a Film Roll on it immediately if you choose.
Tabloid — headhunt

Hostile Takeover

Choose one Crew anywhere on the table. That player rolls 1 D6. On a 1–2, you take the Crew. On a 3+, they keep it and draw 1 card.
